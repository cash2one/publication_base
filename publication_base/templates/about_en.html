From 1991 series of bulletins of the Kyiv’s university of "Mathematician and mechanic", "Physics", "Simulation and optimization of the complex systems" has been reorganized in "Bulletin of National Taras Shevchenko University of Kyiv. Series: physics and mathematics".

The bulletin represents the results of new researches in different branches of mathematics, informatics, mechanics, physicists and radio physics for research workers, teachers, graduate students, engineers and students. Bulletin is printed after recommendations of Academic Council of faculties of Physics, Radiophysics, Mechanics and Mathematics.

Magazine "Bulletin of National Taras Shevchenko University of Kyiv. Series: physics and mathematics" is included to the list of professional editions VAK of Ukraine.
